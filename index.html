Introduction

Heart Failure Awareness is dedicated to spreading knowledge and information about the heart condition, heart failure. According to the CDC website, or the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, Around 5.7 million adults in the US have heart failure and it costs the nation $30.7 billion each year. We hope to build healthier lives and emphasize the importance of having a healthy lifestyle through the information provided throughout the site. Through this site, you will learn:

  • About Heart Failure
  • Warning signs of Heart Failure
  • Treatment options for Heart Failure
  • General tips for staying healthy

Hopefully with the information provided by this website, we can minimize or prevent the number of losses from heart failure and spread the awareness of this condition to as many people as possible.
